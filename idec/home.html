Peplib Generator

In silico directed evolution and denovo generation for

peptide drug powered by AlphaFold2

What We Do

Peplib Generator generates de novo peptide hits library from given target information alone

Our hybrid Al models allow users to explore protein sequence space, create de novo sequence and structures for their desired protein target.

How We Do It

Traditional screening for peptide drug discovery is laborious and time-consuming.

We integrated advanced protein AI models and developed Evolution Algorithm that substitute these expensive computations.
